Approvals

Every Instagram and LinkedIn post I draft lands here before it goes live. You'll see the images, caption, and hashtags for each one. Three options: Approve if it's good to go, Request Revision with specific notes and I'll rebuild it, or Decline to start fresh. Nothing publishes without your sign-off.

📅

Content Calendar

A monthly view of every post — past and upcoming. Color-coded by status so you can see at a glance what's been published, what's scheduled, and what's waiting on your review. Click any post to see the full details.

Your weekly rhythm

  1. I draft your posts and they appear in Approvals
  2. You review each one, takes about 5-10 minutes with a coffee
  3. Approve the ones that are good to go
  4. If something feels off, hit Request Revision and tell me what to change, I'll rebuild it with your notes
  5. Approved posts publish automatically on schedule
